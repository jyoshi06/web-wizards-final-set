About DSU

Learn about our history, vision, and commitment to excellence in education

Premier Engineering Education Since 1999

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University (DSU) is a prominent educational institution located in Trichy, Tamil Nadu. Established with a vision to provide quality education and foster innovation, DSU has grown to become a leading university in the region.

The School of Engineering and Technology at DSU offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented curriculum ensure that students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers.

At DSU, we believe in holistic development, combining academic excellence with extracurricular activities to nurture well-rounded individuals. Our campus provides a vibrant and inclusive environment where students can explore their passions and achieve their academic goals.

NAAC Accreditation

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University has been accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with a 'A' grade, recognizing our high standards in teaching, research, and infrastructure.

NIRF Ranking

Our university has been ranked among the top engineering institutions in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). We are continuously improving our ranking through academic excellence and research contributions.

UGC Recognition

Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under section 22 of the UGC Act 1956. All our programs are approved by the relevant statutory bodies.

Library Resources

State-of-the-art library with extensive resources for learning and research

Central Library

Our central library is a knowledge hub with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. Spread over 5,000 square meters, the library provides a conducive environment for study and research.

The library is equipped with modern facilities including digital reading rooms, group study areas, and 24/7 online access to e-resources. Our dedicated staff is always available to assist students and faculty in their academic pursuits.
